CONVENTION CENTER

Officiate at the centre of well-being.

Spanning over a flourish of 16,000 sq. ft., the Trance Greenfields’ Convention hall is the perfect place to host a gathering, intimate or large, in a luxe affair. Trance Greenfields caters to all occasions with a carpeted conference hall as well as a spacious convention center. A plush three-acre adjoining garden can be adorned exclusively for weddings while the resort’s lawns and halls make for beautiful backdrops to host and share your special moments – be they colourful sangeet ceremonies or expansive nuptials.

With a well-equipped conference hall and boardroom, the resort also caters to the business traveler looking to host conferences and work meetings amidst picturesque surroundings.

A well-groomed scenery for all occasions, official or celebratory.
